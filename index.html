About

I'm Shota, known online as "ZUXROK," and I am an aspiring Game and Web Developer. My drive is to build digital experiences, fueled by a deep-seated passion for coding. While I appreciate the logic of programming, I also find immense joy in the complexities of literature and the thrill of exploring history. I am a dedicated linguist, fluent in German, Russian, and English, and currently mastering Japanese. This blend of technical skill and broad curiosity defines my unique approach to development.

The Spark of My Ambition

A pivotal moment in my life was discovering the anime series Fairy Tail. This show became a constant source of happiness and a powerful drive to always move forward, truly feeling like my salvation. I still watch it today, revisiting episodes to keep that wonderful feeling alive. I believe Hiro Mashima, Fairy Tail's creator, possesses an extraordinary understanding of human connection; his words about creating the series from a place of loneliness resonated profoundly with me, as only someone who has experienced loneliness can truly comprehend it. Fairy Tail has transformed and motivated me in countless ways, shaping my personal growth and creative outlook.

My Development
Toolkit

My technical journey has equipped me with a robust set of skills. I know C#, Angular, JavaScript, ASP.NET, and SQL, alongside HTML, CSS, and SCSS. While I know these languages, I actively use Angular for web development and Unreal Engine for game development. My decision to switch from Unity to Unreal was directly inspired by Edens Zero, another Hiro Mashima series, having a game developed with Unreal. This solidified my belief that mastering Unreal is the most direct path to creating a Fairy Tail game, especially since no Hiro Mashima games have used Unity. This pursuit is about pushing my potential and growing stronger.

My Purpose
in Game Creation

My ambition to develop games is directly fueled by my ultimate dream: to contribute to the creation of a game for the Fairy Tail franchise. LIFEREALM is where I channel my maximum effort. Through this game, I rigorously test my skills, continuously improve as a developer, and showcase my full potential. Every line of code written for LIFEREALM serves as a step towards becoming a stronger programmer, worthy of applying my skills to the creation of new games for the Fairy Tail franchise.

The Path Ahead

This website serves as my personal portfolio, a living testament to my evolving skills and unwavering dedication in game and web development. It showcases my journey, my talents, and the ambitious dreams that propel me forward. Every endeavor is a step towards realizing my aspiration to contribute meaningfully to the digital realm and, specifically, to be closer to the world of Fairy Tail. For a deeper insight into how Fairy Tail continues to shape my future aspirations and my ultimate dream, I invite you to explore the "DREAM" page.

Dream

My ultimate dream is to contribute to game development for the Fairy Tail series. I am currently dedicated to mastering game development in Unreal Engine. While my skills are not yet at the professional level required to work on Fairy Tail game development, my commitment to learning is absolute, and my dream remains unwavering. Achieving this goal will demand significant time and dedication, but the most valuable lesson Fairy Tail has taught me is to never surrender hope and to always keep fighting. The Fairy Tail series has inspired some truly remarkable games. However, I believe there is immense potential for even more innovative and wonderful interactive experiences to be created within its universe. Since I began watching Fairy Tail, I've always envisioned a future where I could be directly involved with the series. Discovering my passion for programming illuminated the path: to leverage my skills and contribute to the vibrant world of Fairy Tail through game development.
